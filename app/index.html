Our Vision

"We love sharing our knowledge"

Netcraft first CEO believed in disclosing and sharing all information we learn from our projects with the UX community, and since then, we follow. In Netcraft Academy's courses we reveal our methodology and work methods as well as give our students a glimpse into Netcraft's work in process.
Our motto is to share everything new we learn and we implement it through our academy, blog and conferences.

Our Students

When we first started we thought that our target audience would be UXers. But as we moved along the way we found out that more and more designers, freelancers, project managers, marketing managers, CEOs and other senior management took interest in our courses.
As the target audience broaden we added management lessons as well, including, how to present a project to decision makers, how to price a UX and design work, how to create an effective portfolio and more.
